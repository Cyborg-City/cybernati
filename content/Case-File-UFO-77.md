# Incident Report: UFO-77

**Date**: July 1947  
**Location**: ||Roswell, New Mexico||  
**Classification**: TOP SECRET

## Executive Summary
On the night of July 4th, a craft of unknown origin crashed into a ranch owned by ||Mac Brazel||. Initial reports suggested a "flying disc," but official statements were quickly changed to a "weather balloon" to avoid ||public panic||.

### Recovered Materials
1.  **Memory Metal**: A lightweight foil that returns to its original shape when crumpled.
2.  **I-Beams**: Small purple symbols were engraved on the surface, resembling ||ancient hieroglyphs||.
