# The Walsingham Sabotage Claim

Anthropologist Lee Miller theorizes that the Roanoke colonists were intentionally stranded as part of a plot by Sir Francis Walsingham, Secretary of State to Queen Elizabeth I.

## Proponent
- **Lee Miller**: Anthropologist and author of *Roanoke: Solving the Mystery of the Lost Colony* (Penguin Books, 2000).

## Premises
- **Targeting Raleigh**: Walsingham sought to sabotage Sir Walter Raleigh’s royal patent for land settlement.
- **Stranding**: The colonists were "intentionally stranded" to ensure the failure of the expedition.
- **Suppression**: The lack of royal records regarding the disappearance may be due to administrative de-indexing or suppression by Walsingham's office (the Office of Continuity).

## Citations & Sources
- *Roanoke: Solving the Mystery of the Lost Colony* by Lee Miller.
