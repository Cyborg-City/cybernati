# Maury Island Incident (1947)

**Date**: June 21, 1947 (Three days before the Arnold sighting)
**Location**: Maury Island, Puget Sound, Washington

## Summary
Two harbormen (Harold Dahl and Fred Crisman) reported seeing six donut-shaped craft. One craft reportedly "spewed" a white lightweight metal and a dark slag-like substance that fell onto their boat, killing a dog and injuring a worker.

## The B-25 Disaster
Kenneth Arnold was asked by [[PER-0007-raymond-palmer]] to investigate. Arnold contacted the Air Force, and two intelligence officers (Capt. William Davidson and Lt. Frank Brown) arrived to collect the debris. Their B-25 Mitchell aircraft crashed on the return flight, killing both officers.

## Strategic Anomaly
The Air Force officially declared Maury Island a "hoax," but the event remains a cornerstone of UFO lore because it marks the first appearance of "Men in Black" style intimidation. Arnold later reported being threatened by a government operative shortly after this case closed.
