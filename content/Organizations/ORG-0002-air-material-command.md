# Air Material Command (AMC)

The **Air Material Command** was the predecessor to the modern Air Force Materiel Command. In 1947, it was the central hub for all UFO reports.

## Key Actions
- **The Twining Memo (1947)**: LTG Nathan Twining, AMC Commander, authored a secret report stating that the UFO phenomenon was "real and not visionary or fictitious."
- **Institutional Redaction**: AMC established the dual-track policy of investigating UFOs seriously in secret while publicly downplaying them as "mirages" or "hoaxes."
