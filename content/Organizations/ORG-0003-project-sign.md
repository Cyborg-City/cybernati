# Project Sign

**Project Sign** was the first official U.S. Air Force investigation into unidentified flying objects, established in late 1947.

## The 1948 "Estimate of the Situation"
The project's most significant output was a top-secret report titled the "Estimate of the Situation." This report concluded that UFOs were not Soviet technology, but likely of extraterrestrial origin.

## Strategic Erasure
The "Estimate of the Situation" was rejected by Air Force Chief of Staff General Hoyt Vandenberg due to a "lack of physical evidence." The report was subsequently ordered burned and the project was reorganized into the more debunking-oriented Project Grudge.
