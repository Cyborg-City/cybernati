# AARO (All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office)

The **All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO)** is the modern U.S. government office tasked with investigating UAPs.

## The Historical Redaction
In early 2024, AARO released a historical review of UAP sightings since 1945. The report concluded that there is "no evidence" of extraterrestrial technology and attributed historical cases—including the [[EVT-0006-mt-rainier-sighting|1947 Arnold sighting]]—to misidentified conventional objects or atmospheric phenomena.

## Strategic Observations
Critics argue that AARO serves as a modern extension of the "Project Grudge" philosophy, focusing on debunking rather than investigation to maintain institutional secrecy.
