# Albert Einstein

Physicist who argued against Bohr's complementarity principle during the 1927 Solvay Conference debates. Believed it should be possible to measure a photon's path without affecting its wave-like behavior, a hypothesis directly tested and disproven by MIT's 2025 experiment.
