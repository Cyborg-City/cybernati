# Kenneth Arnold

Kenneth Albert Arnold (1915â€“1984) was a private pilot and fire suppression equipment salesman who became "Patient Zero" for the modern UFO phenomenon.

## Mt. Rainier Sighting (1947)
On June 24, 1947, while flying his CallAir Model A from Chehalis to Yakima, Arnold witnessed nine "lustrous, delta-shaped objects" flying in formation. He described their motion as "skipping like saucers across water," a metaphor that the press transformed into the label "Flying Saucer."

## Later Theories
In his later years, Arnold diverged from the mainstream "extraterrestrial craft" narrative, proposing that UFOs were actually biological organisms living in the upper atmosphere ([[PAT-0002-space-animal-hypothesis]]).

## Strategic Anomaly
Arnold's family reported significant government intimidation following his investigation into the [[EVT-0007-maury-island-incident]]. His daughter, Kim Arnold, claimed the family felt "threatened for the rest of their lives" by government operatives.
