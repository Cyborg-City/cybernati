# Jellyfish UAP

The **Jellyfish UAP** refers to a specific class of anomalous objects first brought to public attention via leaked military footage from Iraq.

## Anatomical Traits
- **Appendages**: The object features multiple stiff, non-moving "tentacles" or "legs" hanging from a central mass.
- **Spectral Cloaking**: Reportedly invisible to the human eye and standard optical cameras. The object was only tracked using **FLIR (Forward Looking Infrared)** sensors.
- **Medium Transversal**: Observed moving seamlessly from the air into water and back out again without a change in velocity or visible propulsion.

## Primary Signal: Al Taqaddum, Iraq (2017)
Captured by a Force Protection Aerostat (moored sensor balloon) near Al Taqaddum Air Base.

## Institutional Redaction
The **AARO (All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office)** officially assessed the object as a "cluster of balloons." This explanation is widely rejected by the sensor operators due to the object's steady velocity and its reported submersion in water.
