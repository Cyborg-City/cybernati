# The Cybernati™ Ingestion Loop (v4)

This protocol defines the 4-stage lifecycle for intelligence compilation.

## Step 1: /ingest (The Forensic Extraction)
The Clerk extracts the "Ground Truth" from raw sources and stages it in `Clippings/INGEST-[DATE]/`.
- **Atomic Drafts**: People, Organizations, Evidence.
- **`report.md`**: Summary through the three mechanical lenses (Factual, Institutional, Systemic).

## Step 2: /research (Verification & Enrichment)
The Clerk identifies gaps and seeks external validation.
- **Search**: Scans for images, videos, and primary source citations.
- **Enrich**: Updates drafts with verified data and media links.

## Step 3: /synthesize (The Narrative Hub)
After discussion, the Clerk drafts the entry point.
- **The Feynman Standard**: Simple language and analogies.
- **4MAT Structure**: (WHY, WHAT, HOW, WHAT IF).
- **Entry Point**: Drafts a Dossier (DOS) or Internal Memo (MEM).

## Step 4: /finalize (The Merge)
Once reviewed, the Clerk merges the packet into the permanent `content/` folders.
- **ID Assignment**: Final `PREFIX-####` sequence.
- **Linking**: Finalize Wikilinks.
- **Cleanup**: Delete the staging folder.
