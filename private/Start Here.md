Welcome to the vault. As a Cybernati Operative your objective is to observe and catalogue world events and anomalies.


## The Clerk

"The Clerk" is your AI helper.  Start a conversation with Gemini in the terminal. The clerk will look at sources in the `Clippings/` directory. The Clerk will then begin to summarize and extract "entities" (people, places, events, etc...). You can then have a conversation about the findings. 

You do not have to accept what the Clerk writes. You are free to write and rewrite. You can change the format and do what ever you like. Your only goal is to extract "entities" (nodes) and add edges to create an atomic knowledge graph.

**To begin the process:**

1. You can begin the process by using the [Obsidian Web Clipper](https://obsidian.md/clipper) to add articles to the vault in the `Clippings/` directory. 
2. Use the command `/ingest` in your chat with Gemini to begin the process of extracting "entities".
3. `/research`
4. `/sythesize`
5. `/finalize`
