 Most topics will start from `webclippings/` and should be processed and organized into these groups. 
 
- **People** → recurring actors
- **Organizations** → systems of power
- **Places** → geographic anchors
- **Events** → moments in time
- **Claims** → narratives/hypotheses
- **Evidence** → sources/documents
- **Patterns** → cross-case insights
- **Dossiers** → curated synthesis
- **Timelines** → chronology
- **Internal Memos** → Any articles or notes written by Cybernati Agents 

the vault should have:

- clear top-level buckets
- atomic notes
- lightweight hierarchy
- browsing by intuition
- link-first growth
